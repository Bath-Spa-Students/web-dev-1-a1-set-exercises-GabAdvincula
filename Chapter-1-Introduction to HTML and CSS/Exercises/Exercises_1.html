FILIPINO

ADOBO

CHICKEN

Lets Start Cooking

Description

Discover the rich flavors of Filipino cuisine with our authentic Chicken Adobo, a delightful blend of succulent chicken simmered in a savory mix of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices. Each bite offers a perfect balance of tangy, salty, and umami goodness, making it a favorite comfort food that brings the warmth of home to your table. Experience the taste of tradition with our mouthwatering Chicken Adobo, where every meal is a celebration of Filipino culinary heritage.

Ingredients

Chicken (2 pounds) (any part)

Dried Bay Leaves (3 pieces)

Soy Sauce (4 tableppons)

Ingredients

White Vinegar (6 tablespoon)

Garlic (5 cloves)

Water (1 1/2 cups)

Ingredients

Oil (for cooking) (3 tablespoons)

Sugar (1 teaspoon)

Ingredients

Salt (1/4 teaspoon)

Whole peppercorn (1 teaspoon)

Method

Step 1:In a large bowl, combine the chicken pieces with soy sauce, crushed garlic, and black peppercorns. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to an hour. For best results, marinate overnight in the refrigerator.

Step 2:Heat cooking oil in a large pot or deep skillet over medium heat. Add sliced onions and cook until they become translucent.

Step 3:Add the marinated chicken pieces to the pot, reserving the marinade. Cook the chicken until it is browned on all sides. This step helps to lock in the flavors and gives the chicken a nice color.

Step 4:Pour the reserved marinade into the pot. Add the bay leaves and a bit of water to ensure the chicken is partially submerged in liquid.

Step 5:Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 20-30 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender. Stir occasionally to ensure even cooking.

Step 6:Pour in the vinegar, but do not stir immediately. Let it simmer uncovered for another 10-15 minutes, allowing the vinegar to cook off slightly and the sauce to reduce.

Step 7:Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning with salt, pepper, or a bit of sugar if you prefer a slightly sweeter adobo. Continue to simmer until the sauce has thickened to your desired consistency.

Step 8:Remove the bay leaves and transfer the chicken adobo to a serving dish. Serve hot with steamed rice.
